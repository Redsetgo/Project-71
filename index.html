Campus Life

School Library

Was Built in 1950

This glorious school library is home to all sorts of books that children in our school enjoy. We are filled with excitment to show you this exquisite library on this website as it has been one of the most popular places in our school and as a industry that teaches, we deeply believe that reading books is important. With that being said, we are also proud to tell the world that all of our students effortlessly read books everyday!

School Auditorium

Was Built in 1969

This bewildering auditorium is one of the biggest standing structures in this school. It can comfortably hold over 2500 students. In this vast and gigantic auditorium a variety of school gatherings and performances take place. Many of our students here think that the sound quality and the comfort levels of this auditorium are outstanding. From the performers side of things there are a lot of props they can use to create a performance, for example backstage there are pianos kept if the performer plans on having a piano recital.

What's New!

  • New Canteen in School

    In this amazing school we have a state of the art canteen that provides a variety of cuisines. We also have additionally furniture that our students, quote on quote think are "very comfortable". Furthermore, in this canteen you have the most luxurious view of the landscaping near our school. In this landscaping, you can see some lakes, deers, and a vast forest containing a lot of trees.

  • New Sports in School

    Previously in our school, we are afraid to say we didn't have a lot of sports on campus. We only had Soccer, Basketball and Tennis. Our students enjoyed playing these sports for a long time but one day statistics showed that these sports in our school were repetitive making our students feel really bored and restricted when playing sports. Therefore, we decided to take action and added more sports to our school. A few examples of the new sports being added are badminton, swimming, baseball, hockey. Each sport has a respective court being built as of the date this article has been published.
